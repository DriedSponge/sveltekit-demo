My Suggested Solution: SvelteKit + TailwindCSS

Pros of SvelteKit


  • Easy learning curve.
  • It's very fast compared to other frameworks.
  • No server side (until we need it!).
  • It's light weight.
  • Simple to setup.

Pros of Tailwind


  • Mobile optimization is easy.
  • It takes the time out of writing CSS classes.
  • Highly customizable.
  • Small build size.
  • Simple to setup.

I'm open to suggestions of alternative solutions!
